🏃 Lifestyle Habits & Tips

General Wellness Guidelines

Hydration

Drink 8–10 glasses of water daily. More if you exercise or during hot weather.

Sleep

7–8 hours of quality sleep reduces stress hormones and improves immunity significantly.

Exercise

30 minutes of moderate activity (walk, yoga, cycling) 5 days a week is the recommended minimum.

Mental Health

Practice mindfulness, limit social media to 2h/day, nurture social connections.

Avoid

Smoking, excessive alcohol, ultra-processed foods, and prolonged sitting (take breaks every hour).

Regular Check-ups

Annual health check-up, blood tests, BP monitoring, and cancer screenings as per age.
